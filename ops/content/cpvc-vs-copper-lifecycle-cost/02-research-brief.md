# Research Brief

## 1. Audience
- Primary audience: Decision-makers comparing CPVC with another material for a real job.
- Secondary audience: Homeowners and procurement teams evaluating trade-offs.

## 2. Search Intent
- Intent type: comparison
- Why the reader is searching: The reader needs to choose between CPVC and another option instead of collecting features with no decision value.

## 3. Reader Problem
- The reader needs to choose between CPVC and another option instead of collecting features with no decision value.

## 4. Content Objective
- State the trade-off clearly and show when each material is the better choice.

## 5. Domain Context
- Comparison pages are used during material selection and need to support a real trade-off, not a feature dump.

## 6. Important Facts and Insights
- Readers need to know where CPVC wins, where it does not, and what job condition changes the choice.
- Comparison pages should not pretend there is one winner in all cases.
- The page becomes more useful when it states the decision logic in plain language after the comparison table.

## 7. Technical Concepts to Include
- use-case trade-offs
- service conditions
- installation implications
- what changes the winner from one project to another

## 8. Practical Use Cases and Field Situations
- Choosing between CPVC and another material for a home or light commercial project.
- Explaining trade-offs during specification or procurement review.
- Helping a buyer understand why two materials are not interchangeable in all conditions.
