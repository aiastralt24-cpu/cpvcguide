# Content Scorecard

Use this scorecard before moving a draft from `polished` to `publishable` or from `publishable` to `indexable-ready`.

Score each area from 1 to 5.

## 1. Answer Quality
- Does the article answer the exact question early?
- Is the answer specific and useful?

## 2. Technical Usefulness
- Does the page include real plumbing or installation judgment?
- Does it help with a real decision, not just definitions?

## 3. Practical Examples
- Does the draft use field situations, use cases, or common mistakes where useful?

## 4. Trust Language
- Does the article avoid overclaiming?
- Does it explain limits and conditions clearly?

## 5. AEO Extractability
- Is the answer easy to quote, summarize, or extract?
- Are headings and FAQs clear and direct?

## 6. Duplication Risk
- Is the article clearly different from nearby topics?
- Does it deserve to rank on its own?

## Suggested Thresholds

- Below 18: not publishable
- 18-23: publishable with revisions
- 24-27: publishable
- 28-30: candidate for `indexable-ready`
