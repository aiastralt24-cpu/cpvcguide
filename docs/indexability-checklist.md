# Indexability Checklist

- The page is already approved for publication
- The article is technically accurate and safe to quote
- The page is differentiated from nearby topics
- The article is strong enough to represent the topic in search
- The opening answer is clear enough for AI extraction
- Any standards, safety, or compliance claims are validated
- The page is not better handled as a support FAQ or section inside another article
- Internal links and FAQ support are in place
- The page is approved for `indexable: true`
