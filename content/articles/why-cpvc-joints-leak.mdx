## What a leak is really telling you

A leaking CPVC joint usually points to a process problem or a stress problem. Good troubleshooting separates those two possibilities early so the repair approach matches the actual failure mode.

## Process-related causes

Bad fit-up, contamination, rushed assembly, and cure-related mistakes can all weaken a joint. The error often begins before the cement is even applied.

## Stress-related causes

Even a decent joint can leak when the system pulls it out of alignment, leaves it poorly supported, or places ongoing load at the fitting.
