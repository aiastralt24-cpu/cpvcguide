## What support spacing actually does

Support spacing is not just about appearance. It controls sagging, helps maintain line geometry, and reduces unnecessary load concentration on fittings and joints.

## Why installers need context

A single spacing number without pipe size or service condition is not enough. Good guidance ties spacing to the actual conditions of use and explains what poor support tends to damage first.

## How this page helps the cluster

Spacing is one of those concepts that sits between installation and troubleshooting. It belongs in both conversations because poor support often shows up later as stress-related field problems.
