## The practical goal of joining

A joined CPVC system should behave like a continuous flow path, not like two separate parts held together by surface adhesive. That is why the joining process depends on preparation and sequence.

## Core joining steps

Cut the pipe squarely, prepare the surfaces, verify fit, apply the correct solvent-cement method, and assemble without introducing twist or delay that breaks the process window.

## Why leaks show up later

Many bad joints do not fail immediately. They show up after pressure, heat cycling, or movement exposes weak preparation, poor fit-up, or stress at the fitting.
