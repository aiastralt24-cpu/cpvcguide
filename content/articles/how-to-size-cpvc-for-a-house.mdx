## Start with the system, not the pipe

Sizing is not a branding exercise or a single chart exercise. It starts with the demand profile of the house, the fixture layout, the available pressure, and the geometry of the run.

## What the sizing method must consider

A useful sizing page should talk about trunk and branch logic, the effect of elbows and long runs, and why a domestic system may need different sizes at different points.

## Why this page belongs in the standards lane

Sizing sits between engineering logic and practical installation. Readers who need sizing advice usually also need help interpreting terms like CTS, derating, and pressure behavior.
