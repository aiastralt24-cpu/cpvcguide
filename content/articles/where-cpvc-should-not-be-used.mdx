## Why exclusion guidance matters

Readers trust a technical resource more when it explains limits clearly. A page like this prevents the site from sounding like a category cheerleader and helps professionals avoid lazy specification habits.

## Typical exclusion scenarios

The most common red flags involve heat conditions beyond domestic hot-water service, exposure environments that were not part of the intended design, and transitions or mechanical details that create avoidable stress.

## How this page should be used

This is not a fear page. It is a decision page. Readers should leave understanding where they need to slow down, ask a more specific question, or compare materials more carefully.
