## What belongs in this hub

Installation pages should focus on method, sequencing, and the causes of avoidable installation problems. They should explain not only what to do, but why the step matters for long-term system performance.

## What good looks like

A strong installation page is specific, grounded in field practice, and written so a plumber or supervisor can quickly confirm a method without reading through sales copy.

## Next-step routes

Readers moving through this hub should be able to move directly into troubleshooting and standards pages when an installation topic touches pressure testing, spacing tables, or joint failures.
