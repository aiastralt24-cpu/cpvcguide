## Why this hub matters

Technical trust is not built by claiming compliance. It is built by helping readers understand what a standard means, why a pipe size changes, and how engineering terms affect actual design choices.

## What to include

Standards explainers, sizing primers, and glossary-linked definitions belong here. Pages should move from term to consequence, not term to jargon.

## What success looks like

A reader should be able to move through this hub and come away with more confidence about how to interpret CPVC markings, sizing decisions, and system limitations.
