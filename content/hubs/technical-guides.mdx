## Why this hub matters

Readers often search CPVC through technical constraints, not definitions. They want to know if CPVC can handle hot water, whether pressure changes with temperature, and where the material is not the right choice.

## How pages should behave here

Pages in this hub should surface the direct answer first, then explain what the number or limit means in practice. Technical content is most useful when it helps a reader make a better material or design decision.

## Internal linking logic

Every page in this hub should link into installation and standards pages when the performance question depends on support spacing, derating, or compliance context.
