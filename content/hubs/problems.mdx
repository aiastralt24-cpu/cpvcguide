## Why readers come here

Troubleshooting traffic is high-intent and trust-sensitive. Readers usually arrive after something has already gone wrong, so they need clear diagnosis and realistic next steps rather than broad category marketing.

## Editorial rule

This hub should stay especially strict on tone. Failures and troubleshooting pages must remain descriptive, practical, and transparent about likely causes, limitations, and repair decisions.

## Linking behavior

Troubleshooting pages should point back to installation, technical-guides, and glossary pages so readers can understand underlying causes such as overtightening, thermal movement, or poor support spacing.
