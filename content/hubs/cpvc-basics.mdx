## What this hub does

This hub anchors early-stage CPVC learning. It is the starting point for readers who need a clear definition, a practical understanding of where CPVC is used, and confidence that the site will stay technical instead of promotional.

## What belongs here

Definition pages, plain-English explainers, and first-step decision pages belong here. The goal is not to repeat every technical detail, but to build enough understanding for readers to move into more specific pages on performance, installation, and compliance.

## How it should be used

Use this hub as the entry point for broad CPVC searches, then route readers into narrower pages that answer specific questions in more depth.
