## Avoid simplistic material ranking

This comparison is strongest when it avoids the idea that one material wins every project. Readers need a framework for making a house or building-specific decision.

## The practical questions

The useful questions are about corrosion risk, jobsite process, lifecycle cost, and how the system will perform under the local operating conditions.
