## Definition

Solvent cement is the joining material used in CPVC assembly, but calling it ordinary glue is misleading. The joint works as part of a controlled joining process, not as a casual adhesive shortcut.

## Why this matters on site

This term matters because many installation mistakes start with the wrong mental model. If someone thinks the joint is just "glue," they are more likely to rush preparation, skip discipline, and misunderstand why leaks happen later.

## The common mistake

The common mistake is treating solvent cement like a household adhesive. In plumbing work, the process around the joint matters just as much as the material in the can.
