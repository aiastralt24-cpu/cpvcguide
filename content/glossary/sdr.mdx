## Definition

SDR is part of the geometry language used in pipe discussions. You do not need this page to turn into a formula lesson, but you do need to understand that the term is about how the pipe dimensions are being described.

## Why readers should care

This matters most when you are reading a technical page and the language suddenly becomes engineering-heavy. Once you know SDR is part of the dimensional description, the page becomes easier to follow and compare.
