## Definition

Water hammer describes a pressure surge caused by rapid changes in flow. It is important because piping systems react not only to steady operating conditions, but also to dynamic events.

## Why readers should care

A term like this helps connect troubleshooting symptoms to system behavior instead of blaming everything on the base material alone.
