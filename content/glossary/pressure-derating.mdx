## Definition

Pressure derating is the adjustment of allowable pressure based on operating conditions. In CPVC discussions, temperature is one of the most important conditions affecting that adjustment.

## Why the term matters

This concept turns a raw temperature limit into a real design conversation. Without it, a technical page can sound precise while still being misleading.
