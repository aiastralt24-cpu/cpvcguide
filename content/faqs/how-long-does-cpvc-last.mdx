## Keep durability claims grounded

Readers want a clean number, but a trustworthy answer explains that service life comes from the system as installed and operated, not just the base material name.

## The practical takeaway

The right next step is not to chase the longest headline claim. It is to look at installation quality, operating conditions, and likely stress points in the layout.
