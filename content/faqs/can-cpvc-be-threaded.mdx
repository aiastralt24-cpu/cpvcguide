## What the page should clarify

The useful answer is not a blanket permission statement. It is a warning that threaded details need fitting-specific judgment and careful handling.

## Where readers go wrong

Many readers treat threading as a quick field convenience. The better explanation is that connection design and stress management determine whether the choice is sound.
